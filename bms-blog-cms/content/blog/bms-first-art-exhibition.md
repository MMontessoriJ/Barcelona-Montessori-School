Barcelona Montessori School held its very first Art Show! The purpose of the exhibition was to showcase our students' creativity and artistic talents. The event took place in the Esperança Campus canteen, where students proudly shared their artwork with their families and the wider school community.

It was a wonderful event filled with excitement and appreciation for our students' work. Everyone had a fantastic time, and we are looking forward to organizing more events like this in the future.

A special thank you goes to the FabClub community for their support in helping organize the exhibition, as well as to everyone else who contributed to making the event such a success.
