As part of their English studies, the students in the Adolescent Community prepared their own adaptation of Romeo and Juliet. After weeks of preparation and rehearsal, they decided to invite their parents to attend the performance.

In addition to presenting the play, the students also prepared refreshments for the parents, making the event a truly special occasion. The performance showcased not only their understanding of the text but also their creativity, confidence, and collaborative spirit.
