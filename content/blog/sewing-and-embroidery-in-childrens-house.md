This year, we have been working on a class project inspired by the students' strong interest in sewing and embroidery. We are very proud to share the final result. Many hours of focused work and creativity went into it, as the students enjoyed choosing their own designs and threads. Embroidery helps develop hand-eye coordination, concentration, and fine motor skills, while also encouraging patience and self-expression.
