Planning

This year the Saja class sold roses for Sant Jordi. We started planning months in advance. About a month before Sant Jordi we got put into groups. One group was the finance team. They ordered the roses and calculated all the costs. Another team was the creative team. They made posters, and decorations for the stands. The last group was the logistics team. They made sure that there were enough tables for all the stands, what each stand needed, and got it all distributed out.

Selling

On the morning of Sant Jordi we arrived at school at 8:30. There were three groups of people selling. One at Pomaret, one at Esperanca, and one at Carrer de Mandri. At lunch time the groups at Pomaret and Esperanca came to Mandri and sold there until 4:00. Then the Pomaret and Esperanca groups went back to sell there for pickup.

Sant Jordi rose sales went pretty well this year. The one thing we could have done better is that we could have advertised better.
