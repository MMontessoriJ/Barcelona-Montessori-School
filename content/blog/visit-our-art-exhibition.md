Friday, 12th June is party time at BMS. But before you head off to our Pomaret campus to enjoy the amazing decoration and events that our FAB Club and End of Year Party Committee has put together, we strongly encourage you to stop by our Esperança campus at 16:15 to come and see the wonderful art work our students have been creating this year.

Julia, our Art Teacher, Teresa from FAB, and of course the students, are putting together an amazing art exhibition for you. We can't wait to share it with you all, before we head off for a good old party!
