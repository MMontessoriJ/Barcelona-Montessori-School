Last week, Saja, our Adolescent Community, welcomed our transition students for a few days to experience how the Adolescent Program works. The students were able to take part in the morning work cycle, help set up the lunch tables, and participate in other community responsibilities such as cleaning the kitchen and spending time together on the patio. They thoroughly enjoyed the experience and embraced their new responsibilities with enthusiasm.
