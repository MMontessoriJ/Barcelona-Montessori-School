Our Story — a welcome from our founder

In 2015, Tim — then a trained Early Years Educator looking for the right environment for his own daughter — opened Nido Montessori Barcelona, the first Montessori centre active in the city since 1924. What began with 8 children in a 130 m² space grew to over 40 within three years. Barcelona Montessori School opened in September 2020. Today we serve 195+ children and their families across two campuses, over 3,000 m² of installations and 6,000 m² of land.

Life at BMS

A school that feels
like home.

Home is where you are respected and heard. Before the spaces, before the garden, what defines BMS is how children are treated: as whole people, listened to, taken seriously, with the school adapting to the child — never the other way around.

Respect & dignity
Our Values, Lived

Respected. Listened to. Known.

Every interaction at BMS starts from the same place: deep respect for the child. Adults speak to children at eye level, conflicts are resolved through dialogue, and each child's rhythm, interests and needs shape their path — because we adapt to the child, not the child to us.

Families tell us this is what they feel first when they walk in: children who are calm, confident, and genuinely heard.

Carrer Pomaret 25
Outdoor Learning

6,000 m² of garden — a curriculum in itself.

The Mas Pomaret is one of the oldest Masies in Sarrià. The remaining 6,000 m² of heritage garden surrounds our Early Years and Secondary campuses. Children grow food, observe nature, and spend real time outdoors every day — not as a break from learning, but as the deepest kind of it.

For our Secondary students, agriculture and land-based work are woven into the programme. There is no other urban school in Barcelona — or barely anywhere — with a space like this.

See the campuses
40+ nationalities
Community

A genuinely international, multilingual community.

English is the language that unites our community of 40+ nationalities. Children absorb Spanish and Catalan as a matter of daily life — not formal instruction. Communication is natural, and we celebrate the richness that other languages and cultures bring to our understanding of the world.

BMS is a micro-society. Children learn to navigate complexity, resolve conflict, and support one another in a real, mixed-age community — skills that no classroom exercise can replace.

In-house kitchen
Nourishment

Real food, cooked fresh every day.

Our two in-house chefs prepare seasonal, nutritious meals in our own kitchen every day. Children eat together in a shared dining space — a Montessori practical life experience in itself. Menus accommodate all dietary needs and are developed in partnership with Ametller Origen.

Sitting down together to eat is not logistics. It is community.

Secondary · 12–18
The Adolescent Community

Secondary, the Montessori way.

Opened in September 2025 and growing to 18 cohort by cohort, our Adolescent programme is built around what this age actually needs: real work, real responsibility, and a real community. Students run enterprises, work the land in the Mas Pomaret garden, and connect their academic studies — guided by specialist teachers, including doctorate-level guides in Sciences and Humanities — to projects that matter.

It is the natural continuation of the Montessori journey: the same principles of independence and purpose, scaled to young adults preparing for life beyond school.

Why Barcelona Montessori

Built differently, by design.

BMS is not an adapted conventional school. Every element — from the environment to the staff ratios — is built from Montessori principles from the ground up.

01

Pioneer in Barcelona

Founded in 2015 as the first Montessori centre active in Barcelona since 1924. Ten years of accumulated expertise and community trust that no newer school can replicate.

02

Neuroscience-validated

Montessori's core principles — self-directed learning, mixed-age classes, intrinsic motivation — are now confirmed by leading brain development research. We teach the science and follow it.

03

Truly international

40+ nationalities in one community. English as the shared language; Spanish and Catalan absorbed daily. Children grow up genuinely multilingual and culturally fluent.

04

Partnership with families

Classroom observations, parenting talks and daily records through My Montessori Journal — families see the learning as it happens and are part of the community.

05

Exceptional outdoor space

6,000 m² of heritage Masia garden in central Sarrià. A farm, nature, and daily outdoor life are not extras — they are curriculum, especially for the Secondary programme.

06

AMI-trained guides

Every classroom led by AMI or AMS-certified Montessori guides, supported by subject specialists. Our team spans 20+ countries and holds 30+ Montessori diplomas.

Quick facts

Barcelona Montessori School at a glance

BMS is an English-speaking Montessori school in Sarrià, Barcelona, founded from the Nido Montessori project and serving an international community of more than 40 nationalities. The school operates from two campuses: Pomaret for Early Years and Secondary/Adolescent education, and Esperança for Primary. The programme combines authentic Montessori practice with British curriculum pathways, with an adolescent programme preparing students for IGCSE routes. BMS also hosts Barcelona Montessori Training Center for AMS Secondary I–II teacher training.
