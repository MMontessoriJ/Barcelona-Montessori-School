This month we celebrated a special moment for our Year One children, who will be moving into Primary next year.

It was a simple celebration, each child received a flower and a small diploma, a way of marking how much they've grown this year.

Over their time in Children's House, we've watched these children become more independent, more confident, and more capable. They've learned to focus, to take care of their work, and to take on more responsibility, often without even realising it.

This isn't really an ending. It's a step forward. The children are ready for what comes next: a new environment, new challenges, and new things to discover.

It was a lovely moment to stop, look back, and celebrate together, and we can't wait to see them keep growing in Primary.
